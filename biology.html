Why Study Biology?

Biology helps us understand life on Earth. It explains how living organisms grow, reproduce, interact with the environment, and evolve over time.

From microscopic cells to complex ecosystems, biology reveals the fascinating mechanisms that sustain life.

Interesting Biology Facts

  • 🧬 DNA carries genetic information in living organisms.
  • 🫀 The human heart beats about 100,000 times each day.
  • 🌳 Plants produce oxygen through photosynthesis.
  • 🔬 Cells are the basic building blocks of life.
  • 🐋 The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth.
