🌿 Learn About Plants

Plants are living organisms that produce their own food through photosynthesis and are essential for life on Earth.

🌞 Photosynthesis

Process by which plants make food using sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide.

🌳 Types of Plants

Herbs, shrubs, trees, climbers and creepers based on structure.

🌾 Parts of a Plant

Root, stem, leaves, flowers, fruits and seeds.

💧 Transport System

Xylem and phloem help in transport of water and food.

🌍 Importance

Plants provide oxygen, food, medicine and maintain ecological balance.

🌱 Germination

Process by which a seed grows into a new plant under suitable conditions.
